PORTFOLIO

Hellward

Hellward is a 3D Tower Defense game set in a Hellish landscape in the midst of a war between Angels and Devils. You play as the Archdevil, protecting your land in the last stand against the invading army of angels. To combat them you have to collect resources in the night and set up defenses to prepare for the day when the waves of angels are set to attack.

Castle Ninja

Castle Ninja is a 2D platformer which is being created to help further develop my skills in 2D game creation as well as help me further understand a custom achievement system and intricate shop system.

Toggle & Seek

A 2D platformer made to be played using Mouse & Keyboard or a custom controller designed, made and programmed by me using an Arduino.

Corruption of Arcana Chapter 1
